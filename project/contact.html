Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on a hike?

We recommend bringing the following items on all hikes:

  • Sturdy hiking shoes or boots
  • Weather-appropriate clothing (layers are best)
  • At least 1 liter of water per person
  • High-energy snacks
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Small backpack to carry your items

For longer or more difficult hikes, we'll provide a more detailed packing list when you book.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 7 days before hike: Full refund
  • 3-7 days before hike: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days before hike: No refund, but we may be able to reschedule depending on availability

If we cancel due to weather or other safety concerns, you'll receive a full refund or the option to reschedule.

Are your hikes suitable for children?

Many of our hikes are family-friendly, especially our Easy-rated trails. We recommend:

  • Ages 5+: Coastal Bluff Trail and Redwood Grove Trail
  • Ages 10+: Most Moderate trails for active children
  • Teens: Can often handle our Hard trails if they're experienced hikers

Please contact us to discuss your family's specific needs and we can recommend the best options.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We monitor weather conditions closely and prioritize safety. Our policy is:

  • Light rain: Hikes proceed with proper gear (we can provide rain ponchos)
  • Heavy rain/storms: Hike may be rescheduled or cancelled
  • Extreme heat: May modify route or start time
  • High winds: Coastal hikes may be rescheduled

We'll notify you by email or phone at least 12 hours in advance if changes are needed.
