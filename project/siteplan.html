Pacific Trail Hikes

Site Name

Pacific Trail Hikes

This name represents guided hiking tours along the scenic Pacific coastline and mountain trails. It clearly communicates the geographic focus (Pacific region) and the activity (hiking). The name is simple, memorable, and effective for SEO.

Optional domain availability: pacifichikes.com

Site Purpose

The Pacific Trail Hikes website will serve as the primary online presence for our guided hiking tour business. The site will:

  • Showcase our available hiking tours with detailed descriptions
  • Allow visitors to view and sign up for scheduled guided hikes
  • Provide essential information about trail difficulty, preparation, and safety
  • Display high-quality photography of our hiking destinations
  • Serve as a contact point for customer inquiries and FAQs

Scenarios

Target audience questions that will drive content:

  1. "What guided hikes are available next month and how do I sign up?"
  2. "What should I bring on a moderate difficulty hike in the coastal region?"
  3. "Are there any family-friendly hiking options suitable for children?"
  4. "What's the cancellation policy if weather conditions are poor?"
